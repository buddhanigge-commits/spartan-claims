Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is a public insurance adjuster?

A public adjuster is a licensed professional who advocates exclusively for policyholders — not the insurance company. We inspect damage, review your policy, prepare your claim, and negotiate with the insurance company to maximize your settlement. Studies show policyholders who hire public adjusters receive 747% higher settlements on average.

How much does a public adjuster cost in Las Vegas?

Spartan Claim Resources charges a flat 12-20% of your settlement — significantly less than competitors who charge 25-33%. We only get paid when you get paid. There are no upfront costs and no hidden fees.

When should I hire a public adjuster?

Hire a public adjuster immediately after experiencing property damage — before speaking with your insurance company's adjuster. The sooner we're involved, the better we can document damage, preserve evidence, and build the strongest possible claim.

What's the difference between a public adjuster and the insurance company's adjuster?

The insurance company's adjuster works for them — their goal is to minimize what the company pays. A public adjuster works exclusively for you — our goal is to maximize your settlement. We're licensed, bonded, and legally required to act in your best interest.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Southern Nevada including Las Vegas, Henderson, Boulder City, Pahrump, Mesquite, Laughlin, and surrounding areas in Clark County and Nye County. We are licensed in Nevada (NV #3843937) and Utah (UT #1059087).
